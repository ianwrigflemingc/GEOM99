Web GIS Development - Group Solution

Our Ideal

A web app that displays information about the management of Ontario's forest resources including production facilities, forest management units, and wood harvest reports.

Our Reality

The current data sources for forest resources in Ontario are cumbersome to look through, and are not easily accessible on one geographically referenced application that can be used as a tool to make forestry business and management decisions.

The Consequences

Entrepreneurs looking to start up in the Ontario forestry industry may find it overwhelming to locate and connect to the resources and information available from the various government sources without the app, resulting in a loss of investment in the bio economy.

Our Proposal

An interactive web app that displays Ontario forest resource managment information with pop ups for the relevent attribute data. The app will be an informational tool for those working in, investing in, and managing the Ontario forest industry. Available production facility data, forest management units, and the Available Wood Report from Ontario GeoHub will be used.
